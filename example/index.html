What is Questionable?

Questionable is an amazing free Chrome extension designed to help you search website pages using questions. It's a powerful tool that provides instant answers to your queries right within your browser, without sending any information to external servers.

How Does Questionable Work?

Questionable uses advanced AI transforms that are built directly into the browser. This means that all the artificial intelligence computations happen locally on your computer, ensuring your privacy and security. When you have a question about a specific website page, you can simply activate the Questionable extension and type in your question in the search bar.

Why Should Students Use Questionable?

Questionable is an excellent tool for students of all ages. It allows you to search for information on a website page using natural language questions, making it easy to find answers quickly. Whether you need to research a school project, study for an exam, or satisfy your curiosity, Questionable is here to help.

Key Features of Questionable

  • Simple and Intuitive: Questionable's user-friendly interface makes it effortless to search website pages using questions.
  • In-Browser AI Transforms: All the AI processing is done directly on your computer, ensuring privacy and faster results.
  • Instant Answers: Get real-time answers to your questions within seconds, without leaving the website you're browsing.
  • Enhanced Learning: Students can use Questionable to enhance their research skills and find accurate information effectively.

How to Install Questionable?

Installing Questionable is a breeze! Just follow these simple steps:

  1. Open the Chrome web browser on your computer.
  2. Go to the Chrome Web Store.
  3. Search for "Questionable" in the search bar.
  4. Click on the "Add to Chrome" button next to the Questionable extension.
  5. Once the installation is complete, you'll see the Questionable icon in your browser's toolbar.
  6. Click on the Questionable icon to activate the extension and start searching website pages with questions!

That's it! You're now ready to explore the world of Questionable and unlock a new way of searching website pages.
